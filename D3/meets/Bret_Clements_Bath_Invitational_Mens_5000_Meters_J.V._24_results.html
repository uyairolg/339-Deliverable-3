Date: Sat Sep 7 2024
Comments: Ann Arbor Skyline’s JV boys placed 2nd out of 15 teams. Leading the team was Enshu Kuan who finished 3rd with a time of 18:13.19 followed closely by Dylan Hanley in 7th with 18:25.13. Raphael Fournier (11th 18:46.09 PR) Jackson Cichewicz (12th 18:51.99 PR) and Henry Strait (15th 18:58.77) rounded out Skyline’s top five. Other runners who set personal records include Marlowe Hartnett Elliot Daley Emmett Strait Joowon Lee and Lawrence Wang.
